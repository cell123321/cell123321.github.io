Client and Business Contact Privacy Notice

Additional information for client representatives, prospective clients and business contacts.

How business-contact information is used

We process business contact details and correspondence to respond to enquiries, prepare proposals, deliver recruitment and consulting services, manage contracts, communicate about assignments, administer accounts and maintain professional relationships.

Recruitment briefs and third-party information

Clients should avoid including unnecessary personal information in recruitment briefs. Where a client provides personal information about another person, the client should ensure it is permitted to do so and that appropriate transparency information has been provided.

Lawful bases and retention

Processing is generally based on steps taken before entering a contract, performance of a contract, legitimate interests or legal obligations. Records are retained according to the business relationship, contractual requirements and applicable legal obligations.

For further information, see the full Privacy Notice or contact info@optimasolutionsintl.com.
