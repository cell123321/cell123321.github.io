# How to add jobs

1. Open `jobs.json` in your GitHub repository.
2. Click the pencil icon.
3. Copy one complete job entry, including its opening and closing braces.
4. Paste it after the previous entry and put a comma between the two entries.
5. Replace the ID, title, location, workplace, industry, type, salary, closing date, summary, responsibilities, requirements and benefits.
6. Click **Commit changes**.

Every `id` must be unique. Use lowercase words separated by hyphens, for example `finance-manager-cork`.

To remove a job, delete its complete entry from `jobs.json`.

The Apply button currently sends the candidate to your existing CV submission form. To store applications automatically, the next step is connecting that form to Odoo Recruitment.
